Solutions We Provide

Installment Agreement

This solution is a contract between the taxpayer and the IRS where the taxpayer agrees to make regular monthly payments to the IRS.

Offer in Compromise

This solution is an agreement between the taxpayer and the IRS that allows the taxpayer to settle their tax debt for less than the amount owed.

Penalty Abatement

This solution is an IRS program where the IRS removes some or all of the accumulated penalties from the tax debt.

Innocent Spouse Relief

This solution is an IRS tax relief program through which a spouse can claim ignorance of an understatement of due taxes.

Current Not Collectible

This solution is a program offered by the IRS for taxpayers experiencing an economic hardship.

IRS Appeals

This solution can help a taxpayer who disagrees with the assessed tax debt amount, or the IRS’ determination of their IRS program application follow through the appeals process.
